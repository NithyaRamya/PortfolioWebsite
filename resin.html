Resin Art Projects

Creative projects capturing precious memories and beautiful designs using resin techniques.

Preserving Mangalsutra in Resin

This project involves preserving a traditional Mangalsutra within clear resin to create a beautiful keepsake. Resin encapsulation protects delicate jewelry and transforms it into a lasting decorative piece.

Steps Followed:

  • Clean and dry the Mangalsutra thoroughly
  • Prepare a silicone mould suitable for the pendant size
  • Mix clear epoxy resin according to instructions
  • Pour a thin layer of resin into the mould and place the Mangalsutra
  • Carefully pour additional resin to fully cover the jewelry
  • Remove bubbles using a heat gun or torch
  • Allow resin to cure for 24-48 hours
  • Demould and finish edges for a smooth look

Resin Keychains

Resin keychains are custom-made creations featuring initials, glitter, dried flowers, and pigments. They are lightweight, durable, and perfect for personal use or gifting.

Steps Followed:

  • Select silicone moulds for letters or shapes
  • Mix resin with chosen colors, glitter, or dried flowers
  • Pour resin carefully into the mould cavities
  • Remove air bubbles using a heat gun
  • Allow resin to cure for 24-48 hours
  • Demould pieces and attach keychain hardware
  • Polish or sand edges if needed for a smooth finish

Small Mould Resin Pieces

Small resin moulds are perfect for creating miniature art pieces such as charms, fridge magnets, or décor items. They’re excellent for experimenting with color combinations and embedments.

Steps Followed:

  • Choose silicone moulds in desired shapes
  • Mix resin with pigments, glitters, or mica powders
  • Pour resin slowly into moulds
  • Remove surface bubbles using a heat tool
  • Allow resin to fully cure
  • Demould carefully to avoid breaking delicate pieces
  • Finish with sanding or polishing for shine
