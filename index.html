Nithya Baskaran

Software Manual Testing Engineer

I thirst for knowledge and new challenges. I needed a profession that allows me to constantly self-develop and use my talent to find errors. That's how I changed my life, choosing to be a software tester. I'm passionate about mentoring newbies in my project in tech and advocating for diversity in the industry. I'm eager to contribute my skills and experience to cutting-edge tech solutions and collaborate with forward-thinking teams.

I’m a tech-focused Infrastructure Support Analyst and Manual Tester with over 6 years of experience.

I graduated from Velammal Engineering College, Chennai, with a Bachelor's Degree majoring in Electronics and Instrumentation.

I started my career as an IT Analyst providing infrastructure support and access management for various client applications. Later, I worked as an Analyst at an MNC, managing over 5 applications, handling licensed users, knowledge transfers, and backlog management. Currently, as a Senior Quality Engineer, my responsibilities include performing UI testing and production validation across mobile apps, mobile browsers, and web browsers, defect tracking, and collaborating closely with developers for defect analysis.

Moving forward, I hope to expand my experience across different industries.
